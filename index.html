About Me

Hi I'm Mara (rhymes with Sarah)! I'm a senior at University of Michigan studying computer science engineering. I'm excited to use my skill in programming and design to help the world.

At Michigan, I've tried to take advantage of all it has to offer by going to sporting events, attending Michigan theatre and symphonies, joining many clubs and organizations and receiving a broad education. I'm passionate about not only becoming a good coder, but expanding my mind to explore different cultures and phenomena. To do this I've taken a wide variety of classes from other disciplines of engineering, like mechanical and civil, to learning about meditation, planets and moons and greek methology.

Last summer, I had an internship at Uptake as a full stack developer, mostly doing backend work. I learned a lot about Apache Kafka and Docker. I also have a lot of experience doing frontend work from previous internships, classes at Michigan, and side projects.

I'm originally from a small suburb of Boston, Weston, where I graduated from Weston High School. I took my first coding class my senior year of high school and fell in love. It's always been a fun, challenging game or puzzle. I use to take breaks from my english homework by coding. My favorite part about persuing a career in software development is that I know I will always be challenged.
